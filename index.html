How It Works

1

Search & Discover

Doctors search the clinical catalog by skin concern, condition, ingredient, or natural language — powered by AI and grounded in verified data.

The Platform

What Veyderm Does

A clinical intelligence platform for dermatologists — combining AI-powered product discovery, evidence-based treatment planning, and streamlined clinic operations in one system.

AI-Powered Treatment Plans

Describe your patient's condition and Veyderm's AI recommends clinically verified products — with evidence levels, contraindications, and safety data. Build a complete treatment plan in minutes, not hours.

Clinical Product Intelligence

Every product profiled across 14 clinical dimensions — INCI ingredients, evidence levels, pregnancy safety, drug interactions, and regulatory status — verified against EU and UAE standards.

Clinic Operations & Ordering

Request products from authorized distributors, manage clinic inventory, and keep your procurement audit-ready — all integrated into the same platform your clinical team already uses.

For Healthcare Professionals

Built for the Way Clinics Actually Work

Whether you're choosing products for a patient or managing clinic inventory, Veyderm gives you the data and tools you need.

Evidence-Based Product Selection

Search by skin concern, condition (ICD-11 coded), skin type, or active ingredient. Every product shows its clinical evidence level graded on the OCEBM hierarchy.

Safety Contraindications at a Glance

Pregnancy safety, allergen flags, drug interactions, and formulation exclusions displayed on every product — sourced from EU safety assessments and INCI data.

Authorized Distributors Only

Every product is supplied through authorized distributors, ensuring supply chain integrity, authentic products, and clear commercial accountability.

Multi-Clinic, Multi-Role Access

Doctors practicing at multiple clinics access each from a single login. Procurement and clinical roles are cleanly separated with appropriate permissions.
